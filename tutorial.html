Summary

  • - Puzzles are games/toys, designed to test your knowledge.
  • - There are many types of puzzles: logic, math, cipher, jigsaw...
  • - A1Z26 translates your letters to its position in the alphabet.
  • - Atbash is the opposite of your alphabet.
  • - Caesar shifts your letters forward or backward in the alphabet.
  • - Vigenere is Caesar with multiple shifts.
  • - Morse is represented by dots and dashes.
  • - Semaphore is represented by two flags' position in 8 directions.
  • - Transposition is moving your letters without changing it.
  • - Tips:
    • + Gather and analyze information
    • + Search online
    • + Think laterally
