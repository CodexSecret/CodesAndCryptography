Welcome to Codes and Cryptography!

Welcome! I made this website, to teach people about the world of puzzles, and how to solve them! The purpose of this website is to open up the world of puzzles, and problem solving to the rest of the world, so that we can all enjoy it! Puzzles help to train and stimulate your brain to understand more about what can be hidden around you!

Puzzles?

Want to try your hand at some puzzles? Or do you want to understand how some puzzles work! The experience will help you in the future with other puzzles, and understanding how it works!

Want to learn?

Some people may ask, how do I solve puzzles? And I don't know where to start? Not to worry! We have a tutorial page, which includes 4 chapters, and a quick summary if you just want to get in the action!

Problems? Bugs?

If there are any problems, you can contact me via the contact form!

Remember to enjoy, and get ready to stimulate your brain. Always remember, deductions always begin with trivial things. Good luck!
