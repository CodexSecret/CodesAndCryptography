Chapter 3

Hermes turned out to be evil in 14. Maybe I would understand things better if I understood roman numerals...

Answer: Fandaniel

Explanation: Changing 14 into Roman Numerals, you get XIV. After you search Hermes with XIV, you get Fandaniel, a character also known as Hermes, who was a secondary antagonist in XIV.

Chapter 4

King of Kings from Percy; 42, 29, U, 18, 75, 44, 1, 97, 22

Answer: Causation

Explanation: King of Kings is referring to the poem, Ozymandias, which is a poem written by Percy Bysshe Shelley. Using those numbers like a book cipher, you can spell out the word CAUSATION.
